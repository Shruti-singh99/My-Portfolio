Beyond work

What makes me, me.

A lady was staring at a poster I had made. She turned and asked, "Ye sab course seekhate hain kya idhar?" — and then she became 1 of 8.5 lakh students of Yuva Parivartan.

YES Foundation Fellowship, 2019. I joined Yuva Parivartan, an NGO fighting youth unemployment through vocational training. On day two, I sat in a rickshaw wrapped in posters, mic in hand, driving through the slums of Kerwadi in Mumbai.

I spoke to mothers crying for their children who couldn't find jobs despite having degrees. Over two months I wrote articles, made posters, handled social media — small contributions. But the day that poster brought someone through the door, I understood what real impact feels like. It's what I chase in every product decision today.
